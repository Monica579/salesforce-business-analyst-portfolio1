## Business Requirements Document (BRD)
### Client: South Side Girl Apparel

### Business Problem
Customer and order data is spread across email, spreadsheets, and online tools,
leading to missed follow-ups, inconsistent tracking, and limited visibility.

### Business Objective
Implement Salesforce as a centralized CRM to manage customers, contacts,
and apparel orders from inquiry to completion.

### Scope
**In Scope**
- Accounts and Contacts
- Apparel Orders (Opportunities)
- Reporting and dashboards

**Out of Scope**
- Inventory management
- Accounting integrations

### Success Criteria
- All customers stored in Salesforce
- Orders tracked through standardized stages
- Reporting available by customer type and order status
